Projects

Human Balance in Unstable Environments

This was my final project for DSC 106 at UCSD which is a data visualization class. I worked with three other people to develop a website with visualizations we made anaylizing how much people swayed in different environments.

Dengue Cases in Colombia

This was another project for DSC 106. My group and I created a website to visualize which municipalities in Colombia had the heighest rate of Dengue cases per capita from 2007-2019. We created an interactive map for viewers to learn more about how prevalent Dengue was in each municipality.

Cooking Time Analysis

This was my final project for DSC 80 at UCSD. I analyzed a dataset of recipes to see what affected the amount of time a recipe would take. I considered factors such as number of ingredients, month the recipe was posted in, and the day of the week it was posted on.

UCSD Food Coop Sales Report

As a member of the food cooperative at UCSD, I wanted to analyze our sales data for the 2023-24 school year to find our most popular recipes. I also looked at what quarter we sold the most food in, what weeks of the quarter performed best, and what days of the week we sold the most food.

Monthly Reports for Y Montessori

During my time at Y Montessori I have had the opportunity to work on monthly reports created to analyze the current state of Montessori in the United States. I work with the other interns on the data team using Tableau and Figma to create meaningful visualizations that can be shared with the Montessori community.

Cookie Recipe Time Analysis

Built a database of cookie recipes from Sally's Baking Addiction, created a list of flavors to group the cookies, then analyzed them based off of prep time, cook time, total time, and overall rating using Tableau. The data collection was done using python.
